Empowering Farmers Through Various Initiatives

Featured Schemes

1. Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN)

The PM-KISAN scheme provides direct cash transfer of â‚¹6,000 per year to small and marginal farmers. This financial assistance helps farmers to meet their agricultural expenses and improve their livelihoods.

2. Soil Health Card Scheme

This scheme aims to provide farmers with information about the health of their soil and the appropriate measures to maintain its fertility. Soil Health Cards are issued to farmers for a period of three years.

3. Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)

PMFBY is a crop insurance scheme that provides financial support to farmers in case of crop loss due to natural calamities, pests, or diseases. The scheme aims to stabilize farmers' income and encourage them to adopt modern farming practices.

4. Kisan Credit Card (KCC) Scheme

The KCC scheme provides farmers with easy access to credit for agricultural needs. It enables them to avail loans at subsidized interest rates, helping them purchase inputs and meet their farming expenses.

5. National Agricultural Market (e-NAM)

e-NAM is an online trading platform that connects farmers with buyers and markets. It aims to promote transparent trading and ensure better prices for agricultural products through direct access to market information.
